## section-context
type: text-media
mediaPosition: right
label: Context
image: context-img

ABB E-mobility was evolving rapidly through new products, interfaces and ambitions.

A rapidly evolving brand landscape was falling behind ABB E-mobility’s new product world.

There was drive and innovation in the products.
The brand needed to catch up.

The ABB brand language had to evolve for a much more digital world — creating a completely new visual behavior and feeling for the brand.

## section-tension

label: Tension

The products had evolved.
The brand container had not.

The products pointed forward.
The surrounding experience did not.

No independent digital presence.

Innovation existed.
The system to show it didn't.
