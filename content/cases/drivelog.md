## section-context
type: text-media
mediaPosition: right
label: Context
image: context-img
Cars generate enormous amounts of data. Most drivers never see it.

Drivelog explored a simple idea: what if your car could talk to you?

Not through dashboards and error codes — but through clear insights about driving behaviour, vehicle health and everyday use.

---

## section-tension
type: text-media
mediaPosition: left
label: Tension
image: tension-img
The technology existed. The data existed.

What didn't exist was a coherent idea of what this thing was — product, app, brand, or service?

Without that clarity, nothing could be designed. Nothing could be communicated. Nothing could be sold.

---

## section-decision
type: text-media
mediaPosition: right
label: Decision
image: decision-img
One metaphor locked everything in place: the car talking to you.

Not dashboards. Not diagnostics.

A conversation.

That single idea defined the product, the interface, the brand and the communication — all at once.

---

## section-execution
type: text
label: Execution
The speech bubble became the foundation for a bold and distinctive visual identity.

From the product itself to the app, packaging and communication, every touchpoint spoke the same language.

Every interaction reinforced the same simple promise:

Bring your car to life.
